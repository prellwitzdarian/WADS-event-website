Welcome to Our Community

A Safe Space for our fandom to gather, be ourselves, and express our passion.

What We Do

We organize regular meetups, such as love interest specific birthday cupsleeve events at local boba shops, photo card and scrapbook crafting events, LADS theme related community service events, galas,and much more. Anyone is free to join our events as long as they are participating in creating a friendly and safe enviornment for everyone to enjoy. A lot of events require and RSVP so dont forget to check out the event web page in advance to see if you need to secure your spot. RSVP's also often come along with free merchandise which you can pick up at the event by giving you name at the counter. Dont miss out on super cool and exclusive merch by forgettting to RSVP! We support local artists and creators who often vend at our events. You can buy LADS themed merchandise from them at nearly every evetn we host. We are very anti-AI and do what we can to support human creators and real fans of the game. We're always excited to welcome new members who want to celebrate together.

Meet the Organizer

My name is Raye, and I am the founder of Seattle LADS. I started this group to create a safe and welcoming space for fans to gather, share their passion, and connect with others who also share the love of the game and a particular love interest. It's my mission to keep a constant stream of events for our community in order to end the loneliness that some of us feel in our struggle to connect with others and find people like us. Whether you are a Caleb or Rafayel girlie, a casual fan, or just looking for a fun group to hang out with, we welcome you with open arms and have many events in store for you to enjoy. Each event is centered around a different theme of the game. We hope to see you at one of our events soon!
